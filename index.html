Our Mission

Running Routes locates safe running routes for women to run through all across the world.

Testimonials

"Running Routes is perfect for when I travel! Their routes truly make me feel safe, especially when I run in an unfamiliar city or country." - Karlie Kloss

"I love Running Routes because they provide safe and exciting routes! It is great when I travel, but also when I am running at home." - Emma Brown

The Running Routes application will monitor your time, distance, and heartrate while you run. In addition, you will be able to connect the Running Routes app to your music. Running Routes aims to provide the safest experience. The Running Routes app will feature an emergency alert system that notifies pre-saved emergency contacts, the authorites, and other runners with the click of a button.
